# The hard-level trade is a peak/transient effect, not a floor effect: P-B1's DC-b

The hard-level trade is a peak/transient effect, not a floor effect: P-B1's DC-bias floor is lower while its per-env overshoot mean is higher. The two move in opposite directions, which is what separates an e2 signature from a floor regression.
